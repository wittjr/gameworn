{{ site_name }} lets you sign in using third-party providers such as Facebook and Discord. If you would like to remove the data associated with your {{ site_name }} account, follow the steps below.

Disconnect a Linked Account

To stop {{ site_name }} from accessing your Facebook or Discord profile, remove the connection from your {% url 'socialaccount_connections' as connections_url %}{% if connections_url %}connected accounts{% else %}account settings{% endif %} page. You can also remove {{ site_name }} from your Facebook account under Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites.

Request Full Account Deletion

To permanently delete your {{ site_name }} account and all associated data — including your collections, collectibles, images, and profile information — send a request to heavyuse@pm.me from the email address associated with your account, or include your account username.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
